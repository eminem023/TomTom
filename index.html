TomTom GPS Map Update Guide

Keeping your TomTom GPS device updated ensures accurate navigation, optimal routing, and the latest road data. Follow this guide to update your TomTom maps and firmware easily.

Why Update Your TomTom GPS?

  • Access the latest roads, POIs, and speed limits
  • Improve GPS accuracy and device performance
  • Enhance voice navigation and traffic alerts
  • Fix bugs and improve system stability

What You Need

  • Your TomTom GPS device
  • A computer with internet access
  • USB cable to connect your device
  • TomTom MyDrive Connect software

Steps to Update TomTom GPS

1. Install MyDrive Connect

Download and install MyDrive Connect from the official TomTom website on your Windows or Mac computer.

2. Connect Your Device

Use the USB cable to connect your TomTom device to your computer. The MyDrive Connect application will launch automatically.

3. Sign In to Your TomTom Account

Log in using your TomTom account credentials. If you don’t have an account, you can create one during the setup.

4. Check for Updates

MyDrive Connect will display all available updates. Click “Update All” to start the update process. Do not disconnect your device during this step.

5. Complete the Update

Wait for the download and installation to finish. Once complete, safely disconnect your GPS device from your computer.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Device not recognized? Try a different USB port or reinstall MyDrive Connect
  • Slow update speed? Use a wired internet connection for better stability
  • Need help? Contact TomTom support for live assistance

Conclusion

Updating your TomTom GPS ensures smooth navigation and accurate real-time data. Use MyDrive Connect regularly to check for updates and keep your device performing at its best.
